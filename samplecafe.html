Sample Cafe

Organic Cafe in South Yarra

Background

Sample Cafe is an imaginary cafe providing wholesome dishes, baked goods and variety of beverages made with fresh, nutritious and naturally grown ingredients.

Sample Cafe opened its door in South Yarra 2018 and has grown popular among the locals without its own website.

In 2020, the strict COVID restrictions brought the cafe challenges in staying connected with their loyal customers and growing the business while the physical shop was shut down.

One of the key strategies to resolve the issues was to put their energy into building up a web presence and creating their own web page.

Objectives

The key objectives of building this website was to stay connected with their customers and attract a wider audience online especially important during the lockdown.

The cafe’s existing customers are limited to Melbourne and proximity to the store.

Going online allows the business to achieve greater reach to new potential customers who also believe in nutritious value of organic goods.

Thus, expanding the businesses prospect despite the COVID restrictions.

Design Process

The most important consideration of designing this website was to create an informative and memorable webpage and to ensure that the content is available in a variety of formats, device friendly, easy to read and that the information is helpful, relevant and creates a positive experience.

I started writing the “about” section in order to identify and formulate the business concept and their specialty. Focusing on keywords like “natural”, “warm” and “friendly” that best reflected the brand.

Connecting this essence and keywords, I wanted to also capture the store’s welcoming and friendly environment. These important considerations brought me ideas for the layout and creating the visual content to match these emotions. This flowed into the colour selections, fonts and pictures to be used to invoke the right sentiment reflecting the store best.

Colours

The selected colours were inspired by the ingredients that are most frequently used and popular in the cafe’s menu, tomato, avocado, salmon and eggs. Initially I extracted each colour from a picture of the ingredient and adjusted the colour tint, tone and balance to reflect the brands image.

In contrast with unique Parkside fonts, more ordinary and readable fonts were required for headings and body of content and I found “Yrsa” fonts were a great fit.
