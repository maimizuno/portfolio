About

Hello, Thanks for visiting my portfolio website!
I am Mai Mizuno, a Melbourne based web designer/developer and a passionate IT student at Melbourne Polytechnic.
I was born and raised in Japan and my childhood was greatly influenced by my uncle who was a very skilful carpenter, this inspired my studies in architecture and industrial design and leaded to opportunities working as a commercial building designer, where I was responsible for managing projects from concept to completion, specialising in interior design of restaurants, bars and hair-salons in Kyoto.
During my tenure I was involved with a great variety of different clients from all corners of the world but faced some difficulties communicating (particularly English), as such I made the tough decision to leave a great job in pursuit of improving my English skills and to study abroad and live in an english speaking country, doing so would help immensely evolve my language and overall life skills.
After arriving in Melbourne in 2017, took numerous courses starting with general English subjects, then transitioned across to business, marketing and web design , this provided great opportunities not only to study English, but also to develop a better understand of a different culture and other subjects I was interested in.
During my journey I discovered my true passion and developed a great interest in the structural design of information and communication through digital media. Within this field I am strongly drawn towards coding and programming languages and the endless possibilities enabling me to create almost anything, which I found just fascinating!
As such, I decided to explore and pursue the field of IT and enrolled myself at Melbourne Polytechnic. At the same time, I am looking for opportunities to utilise my skills of designing and coding to build and develop websites while continuing to study.
